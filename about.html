The Story Behind Echo

I'm John Ogletree, a digital creator based in Martinez, Georgia. I build digital experiences that are as functional as they are beautiful.

My Journey

My fascination with the web started early. I remember the thrill of writing my first lines of HTML and seeing them come to life in a browser. That curiosity led me to the Georgia Institute of Technology, where I honed my technical skills and discovered my passion for human-centered design.

Over the years, I've worked with a diverse range of clients and companies, from small startups to established enterprises. Each project has been an opportunity to learn, grow, and refine my craft.

Echo is more than just a portfolio; it's a reflection of my philosophy as a creator. It's about finding the perfect balance between form and function, and building things that resonate with people.

Philosophy

Quality First

I believe in doing things right the first time. Clean code and thoughtful design aren't just luxuries; they're the foundation of a great user experience.

User Centric

Technology should serve people, not the other way around. I start every project by asking who we're building for and what they truly need.

Continuous Learning

The web is constantly evolving, and so am I. I'm always exploring new tools, techniques, and ideas to stay at the forefront of the industry.

Interested in working together?

I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to collaborate with creative people.
